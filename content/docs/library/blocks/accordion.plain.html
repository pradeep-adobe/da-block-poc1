General FAQs

What is Starbucks Reserve?

Starbucks Reserve is a selection of the most extraordinary coffees Starbucks has to offer. Discover Starbucks Reserve at our Roasteries and stores around the world.

Where can I find Starbucks Reserve?

You can find Starbucks Reserve at our Roasteries in Chicago, New York, Shanghai, Tokyo, and Milano, as well as at select Starbucks Reserve stores. Visit our Find a Location page for details.

Can I buy Starbucks Reserve coffee online?

Starbucks Reserve coffees are available exclusively at Starbucks Reserve Roasteries and stores. Visit your nearest location to explore our current selection.

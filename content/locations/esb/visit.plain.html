Visit Us

In a building that is among the world’s most iconic. In a city known for welcoming all. Visit the Starbucks Reserve® Empire State Building® store for a journey of the senses, an experience like no other. An immersive discovery of craft coffee, food and cocktails, carefully curated shopping and more—all perfectly designed to be shared and celebrated together.

Hours

Monday – sunday:

Ground Floor: 6:30AM – 10:00PM

Tasting Room and Experiences (Concourse): 8:00AM – 8:00PM

Coffee & Cocktail Lounge: 11:00AM – 10:00PM

Directions

350 5th Ave
New York NY 10118

(332) 209-4785Get Directions

‍

BY CAR OR WALKING:

Go to 350 5th Avenue at the cross-streets of 5th Avenue and W. 34th Street.

BY SUBWAY:

From 123 or ACE lines, exit the train at 34th Street – Penn Station. From Penn Station, it’s a five-minute walk east to the store entrance.

From the NQRW or BDFM lines, exit the train at 34th Street – Herald Square Station. From Herald Square, it’s a five-minute walk east to the store entrance.

From the 456 or 7 lines, exit the train at Grand Central Terminal. From Grand Central, it’s a 10-minute walk to the store entrance. Travel south on Park Avenue or 5th Avenue and walk west to reach the store entrance.

BY BUS:

There are many bus lines that stop within a 5 minute walk of the Empire State Building. Go to https://new.mta.info/ for the latest bus route information.

FAQ

What can I only get at Starbucks Reserve® Empire State Building® store?

This is a landmark destination designed for discovery with 3 floors of one-of-a-kind coffee, cocktails, food & shopping. You can commemorate your trip with exclusive merchandise only available at Starbucks Reserve Empire State Building®, book a Starbucks Reserve Experience or grab your favorite Starbucks classics to go. These are just some of the experiences that can only be found at the Starbucks Reserve Empire State Building® store.

Do I need to book Starbucks Reserve Experiences in advance?

Because class and seating capacity are limited, we highly recommend booking in advance by clicking the Book Experience Tickets button on our website. If you are unable to pre-book tickets, you can check in with the Concierge on Level 1 to see the upcoming availability.

What is the Tasting Room & Experience Level?

The Tasting Room & Experience Level is located on the Concourse. It is a place to sit down, relax and recharge, chat with your friends and take it all in—in a space created just for this. Exclusive Starbucks Reserve beverages and seasonally rotating single-origin coffees and exclusive, table-side Signature Flights paired with a curated selection of delicious food. Explore the art and science of exquisite coffee with Starbucks Reserve Experiences. With several experiences to choose from, we invite you to learn about best-in-class brewing techniques and mixology skills.

Can I get my favorite Starbucks drink at this store?

Yes, the menus feature classic, customer-favorite Starbucks drinks, such as Caramel Macchiato and Frappuccino® blended beverages, as well as seasonal favorites like Peppermint Mocha, in addition to Starbucks Reserve specialty beverages.

Can you accommodate different dietary preferences or food allergies?

Each menu at Starbucks Reserve Empire State Building features a variety of items suited for various dietary preferences.

You can find all the Starbucks Reserve Empire State Building® menus on our website.

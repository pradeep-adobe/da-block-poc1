Next level food, cocktails, coffee and good times together.

Welcome to Level 2, home to the Coffee & Cocktail Lounge. Yes, this is cocktails at Starbucks. A full bar awaits—stop in for a Starbucks Reserve Espresso Martini before the show, a Negroni on your way down from the observatory, or anytime you’re looking for exclusive coffee creations. Boulevardiers and Roastery Old Fashioneds, to classic cocktails and more, all handcrafted by our expert mixologists in a space as iconic as its setting.

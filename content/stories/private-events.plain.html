Starbucks Reserve Roasteries and stores are the perfect setting for your intimate gathering or grand celebration.

Starbucks Reserve Roastery New York

61 9th Ave
New York, NY 10011
‍ For groups of 10-500

Sleek and inspiring, the nearly 23,000-square-foot New York Roastery sits in the heart of the city’s historic Meatpacking District. Surrounded by the neighborhood’s energy, art and culture, this is truly an unforgettable space and experience. Both workshop and stage, the New York Roastery allows you to witness the art, science and theater of coffee craft and artisanal baking. Featuring an impressive mix of bars and custom details, and hosting groups from 10-500. Gorgeous, custom furniture and soaring spaces abound, with art and details everywhere you look, beginning with a 10-foot, 2000-pound copper Siren keeping watch over it all. The ceiling is covered by an ocean of squares and rectangles, inspired by the building’s exterior and the grid of New York City blocks outside. Host your event in your choice of spaces — from the mixology of our Arriviamo cocktail bar, to the main space and upper and lower areas perfect for gathering.

Starbucks Reserve Empire State Building® Store

350 5th Ave
New York, NY 10118
‍ For groups of 10-500

Discover three floors of coffee craft and innovation in one of the world’s most iconic buildings. The Starbucks Reserve® Empire State Building® store offers a journey of the senses and an experience like no other. A three-story mural opens the space, with beautiful art-deco inspired details and art throughout the store. An immersive discovery of craft coffee, food and cocktails, all in a place perfectly designed for celebrating together. Located at the base of the legendary New York skyscraper, the 23,000-square-foot store plays host to groups from 10-500 in beautifully unique settings. For groups of all sizes, our Coffee & Cocktail Lounge offers a space as iconic as the building it calls home, while more intimate private rooms, and the Tasting Room & Experiences lounge, are also available. For large occasions, the entire space is available for yourself and your guests.

Starbucks Reserve Roastery Chicago

646 Michigan Ave
Chicago, IL 60611
‍ For groups of 10-750

At the top of Chicago’s famed Magnificent Mile sits the largest Starbucks in the world. A five-story, 35,000-square-foot tribute to Chicago’s art, architecture and culture, the Chicago Roastery is truly a spectacular destination — and home for your next event. Groups from 10-750 can experience the craft of coffee in an immersive and thoughtfully designed space made for gathering. All connected by the Midwest’s first curved escalator, and built around a towering 56-foot steel coffee cask. One full floor is dedicated to the art, science and theater of coffee, in the experiential coffee bar. While another full level is home to the artisan Italian Princi Bakery and Cafe. The Reserve Coffee Bar and roaster occupy the first floor, while yet another level is dedicated to the world of inspired cocktails and mixology. The top floor? An outdoor roof deck with singular views of the city and Michigan Avenue.
